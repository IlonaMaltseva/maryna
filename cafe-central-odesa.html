Cafe Central Odessa

  • Project lead/interior designer/architect: Maryna Soloviova
  • Location: Odessa, Ukraine
  • Area: Main restaurant 15 ㎡ summer terrace 121 ㎡
  • Year of realisation: 2021

Brief

The restaurant is located on the main street Daribasovska, which is the heart of Odessa. Interior is performed in the most advanced technologies and exclusive materials. The people of our city and guests enjoy the aesthetics and the dishes which central cafe prepared for them every day.
